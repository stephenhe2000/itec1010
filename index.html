Si Cheng He

I'm a data scientist at Google Research in San Francisco. NeRF.

At Google I've worked on large datasets based on Data Mining, HDR+, VR, and Maps. I did my PhD at UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Jitendra Malik. I've received the PAMI Young Researcher Award.
